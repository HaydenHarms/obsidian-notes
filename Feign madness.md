# Feign madness
People often mistake passion for obsession and obsession for madness. In my experience, and apparently in al-Haytham's as well, not being ashamed of your passions are the best way to get more room to pursue them. The average person will look at someone that is interested in something that is "quirky" and initially think they're just trying to get attention. This makes sense because there are many "performative" people that this is true for. However, those that are actually interested in something aren't in it for the outcome of being interested, but for the thing itself.<sup>1</sup>
